About Dr. Shrestha

Dr. Prashant Shrestha is the first astronaut from Nepal and the first person to land on Mars. With a PhD in Astrophysics, he has revolutionized space exploration and research on interplanetary travel.


He has completed multiple missions including spacewalks and lunar exploration, contributing to human knowledge of extraterrestrial environments and space technology.

Space Missions

Mars Expedition I (2026)

First human mission to Mars. Conducted experiments on Martian soil, atmosphere, and potential habitability for future colonies.

Moonwalk Mission (2024)

Participated in the first Nepalese moonwalk. Conducted lunar geological studies and tested equipment for extended lunar habitation.

ISS Spacewalk (2023)

Performed multiple spacewalks for the International Space Station, maintaining modules and conducting scientific experiments in microgravity.
